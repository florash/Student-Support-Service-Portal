# Student Support Service Portal

🔗 Live Demo: https://your-live-link

---

## Overview

Designed and developed a student support portal to help users find services, understand eligibility, and take the next step when seeking assistance.

---

## Problem

Students often do not know:
- which service to contact  
- whether they are eligible  
- what steps to take next  

---

## Users

- University students  
- International students  
- Staff or carers  

---

## Goals

- Improve service discoverability  
- Provide clear next steps  
- Reduce confusion  

---

## Key Decisions

### Task-based homepage structure
Homepage is structured around user intent rather than organisation structure.

### Separation of services and resources
Services = actions  
Resources = information  

### Single primary entry point
Avoid competing actions.

### Scannable content
Short sections, clear headings.

---

## Accessibility

- semantic HTML  
- keyboard-friendly  
- clear hierarchy  
- plain English  

---

## Outcome

The portal improves clarity, navigation, and usability, and reflects a realistic public-sector support system.
